**Gravity Flip** is a fast-paced 2D endless runner where the player controls a character running through a dangerous corridor filled with obstacles. The only control is **tapping the screen to flip gravity**, switching the character between the floor and ceiling.
 
The objective is to **survive as long as possible** while the speed gradually increases and obstacles become more complex.
 
The game is designed around:
 
 
- **Easy to learn (1 tap control)**
 
- **Hard to master**
 
- **Short gameplay sessions**
 
- **Frequent deaths**
 
- **Highly addictive retry loop**
 

 
This design creates strong player engagement and natural opportunities for **advertisement monetization when players die**.
  
# Core Gameplay
 
## Player Control
 
The player has only one action:
 
**Tap the screen → Flip gravity**
 
When gravity flips:
 
 
- If the character is on the floor → it moves to the ceiling.
 
- If the character is on the ceiling → it moves to the floor.
 

 
The character continues running automatically at a constant forward speed.
  
## Game Objective
 
The goal is to **survive for as long as possible** while navigating obstacles.
 
The player's score increases based on **distance traveled**.
 
Example scoring:
 
 
- Every second survived = +1 score.
 

  
## Difficulty Curve
 
The game gradually increases difficulty through:
 
 
1. Increased running speed
 
2. More frequent obstacles
 
3. More complex obstacle patterns
 
4. Narrower safe paths
 

 
Example progression:

# Player Game Flow
 
## Game Session Flow
 
 
1. Player opens the app
 
2. Main menu appears
 
3. Player taps **Play**
 
4. Game starts immediately
 
5. Player flips gravity to avoid obstacles
 
6. Player dies when colliding with obstacle
 
7. Death screen appears
 
8. Player chooses: 
 
  - Retry
 
  - Watch ad to revive
 

 
 
9. Game restarts
 

 
This creates a short and addictive gameplay loop.

Located at the **top corner**.
 
Pausing the game opens the pause menu.
  
### Visual Feedback Effects
 
To enhance gameplay experience:
 
 
- Near-miss glow when narrowly avoiding spikes
 
- Speed glow when difficulty increases
 
- Screen shake during close calls
 

 
These effects help create excitement and tension.
  
# Obstacles
 
Obstacles are the primary challenge of the game.
 
## Basic Obstacles
 
### Floor Spikes
 
Sharp spikes on the ground.
 
Player must flip to the ceiling to avoid.
  
### Ceiling Spikes
 
Spikes attached to the ceiling.
 
Player must flip to the floor.
  
### Spike Walls
 
Large walls of spikes requiring precise timing.
  
## Advanced Obstacles
 
### Moving Spikes
 
Spikes that move vertically between floor and ceiling.
  
### Rotating Blades
 
Circular spinning hazards placed in the corridor.
  
### Laser Gates
 
Laser beams that activate briefly and block the path.
  
### Narrow Corridors
 
Small safe gaps requiring perfect timing.
  
# Death System
 
A player dies when the character touches any obstacle.
 
### Death Effects
 
To create dramatic feedback:
 
 
- Slow-motion collision
 
- Bright impact flash
 
- Short sound effect
 

 
This makes deaths feel impactful and encourages retrying.
  
# Death Screen
 
After death, the results screen appears.
 
## Displayed Information
 
 
- Final score
 
- Best score
 
- Unlock progress
 
- Retry button
 
- Revive option (watch ad)
 

  
## Revive System
 
Players can choose to watch a **rewarded advertisement** to continue their run.
 
Rules:
 
 
- Revive places the player slightly before the death point.
 
- Only **one revive allowed per run**.
 

 
This encourages ad viewing while keeping gameplay fair.
